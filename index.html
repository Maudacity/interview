My amazing self interview.

November 12th, 2019
Why do I want to be a designer?

I enjoy solving problems, and with my passion for art and design solving those problems creatively is the perfect job.

What worries me about being a designer?

Once college ends, finding work and pushing my portfolio to a point where I have job security

What excites me about it?

The fact I will be creating beauty and artistic solutions for my career!

What aspect of design do I gravitate towards the most?

I find package design, media design in music and film, and illustration the most enticing parts of design.

What role or responsibility does a designer play in our society?

The role of a designer in our society is taking a problem and solving that problem creatively and visually
